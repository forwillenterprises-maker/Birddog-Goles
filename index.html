Process

How It Works

Our acquisition process is straightforward. From your first lead submission to closing day, here's exactly what to expect.

01
Find the Deal

You Identify a Motivated Seller

This could come from driving for dollars, cold calling, direct mail, a personal connection, or any other source. The key is the seller needs to have a real reason to sell — retirement, financial distress, estate situation, divorce, or just tired of owning. Off-market deals are ideal, but listed properties work too.

Driving for dollars Cold calling Direct mail Referrals Broker relationships MLS / Loopnet
02
Submit

Fill Out the Deal Submission Form

Use the Submit a Deal form on this site. Tell us everything — the address, seller's name, contact info, why they're selling, their timeline, what terms they're open to, and any financial details you know. Upload anything you have: rent rolls, T12s, photos, an OM if there is one.

The more detail you give us, the faster we can move. A complete submission with seller motivation, timeline, and financial info gets reviewed in hours — not days.

03
Review

We Evaluate & Respond Within 24–48 Hours

Our team reviews every submission. We look at the deal fundamentals — price vs. value, NOI, occupancy, seller motivation, deal structure possibilities — and make a quick decision. If we want to move forward, we'll contact you directly. If it's not a fit, we'll let you know why.

What we're evaluating
✓ Price vs. market value✓ Seller motivation strength ✓ Cash flow potential✓ Deal structure flexibility ✓ Occupancy & condition✓ Timeline feasibility
04
Negotiate

We Handle the Seller Directly

Once we decide to pursue a deal, our team takes over negotiation and communication with the seller. We'll keep you in the loop as the deal progresses. You don't need to do anything further — just make sure the seller knows someone from our team will be reaching out.

05
Payday

You Get Your Finder's Fee at Closing

When we close on a property you brought us, you receive a finder's fee. The amount depends on the deal size and complexity. We pay at closing — no waiting, no runaround.

💡 The best birddogs in our network submit consistently — even deals that don't close today help us understand what's in the market and often lead to future opportunities.

What We Acquire

Property Types

We specialize in income-producing commercial and residential assets. Here's what we're actively looking for — and what makes a strong submission for each.

🏘️
Co-Living
Actively Acquiring
What we're looking for

Shared housing properties where individual rooms are rented separately. Large single-family homes, rooming houses, student housing, intentional communities, and properties that can be converted to room-by-room rentals. Seller financing strongly preferred.

Strong submission includes
  • Number of rooms / beds
  • Current rent per room
  • Occupancy rate
  • Lease structure (individual vs. master)
  • Zoning / local regulations
🏕️
RV Parks & Campgrounds
What we're looking for

RV parks, campgrounds, and RV resorts — seasonal or year-round. Value-add through adding hookups, improving amenities, raising below-market rates, or adding pull-through sites. Seller financing welcome.

Strong submission includes
  • Number and type of sites
  • Seasonal vs. year-round
  • T12 or annual revenue
  • Current ADR / occupancy
  • Amenities present
🏠
Mobile Home Parks
What we're looking for

Manufactured housing communities of any size. Value-add through filling vacant pads, raising below-market lot rents, implementing utility bill-backs, or improving infrastructure. Motivated sellers a big plus.

Strong submission includes
  • Total pads / occupied pads
  • Lot rent per pad
  • City vs. well/septic
  • Tenant-owned vs. park-owned homes
  • Rent roll
🏢
Multifamily
What we're looking for

Apartment complexes, duplexes through larger buildings. We target below-market rents, value-add through renovations, distressed assets, and off-market estate or distress situations. Any size considered.

Strong submission includes
  • Unit count and mix (1/1, 2/1 etc.)
  • Average rents vs. market
  • Occupancy
  • Year built
  • Rent roll
📦
Self Storage
What we're looking for

Climate-controlled and non-climate-controlled facilities. Value-add through rate increases, improved management, adding units on excess land, or converting underused commercial space. T12 and rent roll essential.

Strong submission includes
  • Unit count and unit mix
  • Climate vs. non-climate
  • Current occupancy
  • T12
  • Street rates vs. in-place rates
🏪
Retail / NNN / Mixed Use
What we're looking for

Single-tenant NNN, strip centers, and mixed-use properties with commercial and residential components. Long-term leases with creditworthy tenants preferred. Distressed or vacant properties considered with upside thesis.

Strong submission includes
  • Tenant names and lease terms
  • Lease expiration dates
  • NNN vs. gross structure
  • Cap rate
  • Any co-tenancy clauses
🌿
Land & Other

Development sites, agricultural land, unique assets, and portfolios. If you've found something interesting that doesn't fit a category above, submit it anyway — we review everything. Include zoning, acreage, utilities available, and any entitlements already in place.
